About Artemis

The Artemis program is NASA’s ambitious initiative to return humans to the Moon and establish a sustainable presence there by the end of the decade. Designed to ignite a new era of lunar exploration, Artemis will lay the groundwork for future human missions to Mars and beyond.

Artemis II: The Next Step

Artemis II: The Next Step

Artemis II is set to be the first crewed mission of NASA’s Artemis program, sending astronauts on a lunar flyby to test critical systems of the Orion spacecraft in deep space.

Serving as a pivotal milestone, Artemis II validates key technologies and paves the way for future lunar landings and exploration – bridging groundbreaking innovation with the next giant leap in human spaceflight.
